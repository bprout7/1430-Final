Welcome to Poseidon Paintball

Poseidon Paintball is the largest indoor playing field, offering rental options for the beginners as well as gear for the more advanced players. Poseidon Paintball offers an air conditioned field and Pro shop during the summer months and a heated field in the winter months, making it comfortable year round to play and enjoy paintball, also offering food and drinks. We take Great Pride in our facilities and will make sure that your experience with us will make you want to return time and time again.

Safety First!

Safety is our number one priority. Please review and be familiar with the rules for our field. If everyone follows the rules at all times we know that you will have the most fun and everyone will avoid injuries.

  1. Mask on at all Times!!! When on the field
  2. Always listen to the Ref
  3. Mask and barrel cover must be on when entering or exiting the field
  4. Barrel cover on at all times when off the field
  5. Mask on at all Times!!! When on the field
  6. No pistol whipping
  7. If you are out, do not speak
  8. Mask on at all Times!!! When on the field

Lets talk about gear!

   Basic gear in paintball includes

  • Mask
  • Marker (the gun)
  • Hopper (also called the loader)
  • & a Tank

All of which come with our rental packages. (see About the Field)
Here are a few tips to get the most out of your equipment, whether you are renting our equipment or using your own.

  • Always keep your gun clean, Our staff can clean your gun between games if you
  • Never pickup paint off the field, it breaks easily and gums up your gun
  • Keep your air pressure between 2 and 3 (thousand) PSI
  • Clean your mask after each game with a wet cloth

How to Play

There are many games that you can play, capture the flag and elimination being the most popular. The rules to these games are very simple. In both games if you are hit anywhere on your body, gun, or mask or if you are tagged, you are out. If you are eliminated you exit the field and stand behind a net called the dead box, until the next game. In Capture the flag there is a single flag in the center of the field. The goal of this game is to retrieve the flag and take it to the dead box on your opponent’s side of the field without getting hit. In Elimination the goal is to eliminate all players on the other team.


Be sure to ask our staff about other games like Zombies, body guard, limited shots, and the Hunger games (for private parties or experienced players)
